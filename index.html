5 Seconds of Summer

How well do you know 5 Seconds of Summer?

5 seconds of Summer, often shortened to 5SOS was formed in late 2011. The band members include Luke Hemmings, Michael clifford, calum Hood and Ashton Irwin. They originally began their careers as youtube celebrities then rose to fame while touring with English-irish boy band One Direction on their Take Me Home Tour. Since 2014, 5 Seconds of Summer have sold more than 10 million albums, sold over 2 million concert tickets worldwide, making them one of the most successful Australian musical exports in history. The band released She Looks so Perfect in 2014 which topped charts in four countries and is also my favourite song.
